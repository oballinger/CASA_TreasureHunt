CASA 2023 Treasure Hunt

Welcome to the 2023 CASA Treasure Hunt! The map above shows a list of landmarks around london, carefully selected for their aesthetic, culturual, or historical significance. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, will be to visit as many of these sites as possible before 3:30pm today.

Each marker on the map is sized according to the number of points that visiting is worth. For example, though Big Ben is worth 2 points, if you make the journey to the Thames Barrier you'll be rewarded with 25 points.


The Rules of the Game

1. In order to receive the points, you must post a picture with the landmark in which ALL team members are visible. You must be reasonably close to the landmark.

2. You must tweet the picture using the hashtag #CasaTreasureHunt2023 and the name of the landmark in question. No tweet, no points.

3. The landmarks on the map are colored by category; you can click on the legend to isolate a specific category. The team with the most landmarks for a certain category will receive an additional bonus:

Category Bonus
Cricket 10
Football 10
Shopping 10
Sporting Heritage 20
Palace 20
Architectural Wonders / Eyesores 25
Big Infrastructure 25
Tourist Tat 25
Musical Heritage 30

4. The game ends at exactly 3:30pm. Tweets posted after this time will not be counted. We will meet at the CRATE Brewery & Pizzeria at 4pm to announce the winner and give out prizes.
